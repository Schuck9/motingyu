I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Advance Well-being and Society Research Platform (AI-WiSe) at The University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Victor OK Li and Dr. Jacqueline CK Lam. Prior to this, I received my master degree in Electronic and Information Engineering with the School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University, Beijing. My supervisor is Professor Lei Ren. I received the B.S. degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from School of Automation and Electeical Engineering, University of Science and Technology Beijing, Beijing, China, in 2021. My research interests include AI for Social Good, Alzheimer's Disease Detection, Domain Generalization.
